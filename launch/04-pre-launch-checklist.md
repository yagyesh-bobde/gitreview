# GitReview — Pre-Launch Checklist (4-week runway)

The launch is won before launch day. Ranking is *relative* — there's no fixed upvote threshold — but for a confident Top-5 finish you want a mobilizable audience of **~400+ supporters** and **200–500 email subscribers** ready to engage on launch day.

> ⚠️ **2025 change:** Product Hunt killed "Coming Soon" / "Notify Me" pages (~Aug 2025) and no longer sends follower notifications. **You must own your audience off-platform** (email list, X, Discord) — PH gives you no built-in notify mechanism or subscriber data anymore.

## Choose your launch day

| Goal | Best day |
|---|---|
| Max signups / you have a strong audience to mobilize | **Tue / Wed / Thu** (highest traffic, more competition) |
| Mainly want the **#1 badge**, smaller list | **Fri / Sat / Sun** (~15% more visit-clicks, fewer serious contenders) |
| — | **Avoid Monday** (crowded with teams who all planned for it) |

Every #1 AI-code-review winner (Trag, cubic) and nearly all top dev-tool launches landed Tue–Thu. Default to **Wednesday or Thursday** unless your list is small.

Treat this as the first of a *cadence* of named-release relaunches — Cursor (9 launches), Warp (5+), Graphite (4) compound followers each time.

## 4 weeks out
- [ ] **Schedule the launch** in PH (you can schedule up to ~1 month ahead) — picks your date and starts collecting followers.
- [ ] Stand up a **Product Forum / `/p/` page** (the new "Coming Soon" replacement) to tease and collect early interest.
- [ ] Add **email capture to the landing page** ("Get notified when we launch on Product Hunt"). This list is your launch-day engine — PH won't notify anyone for you.
- [ ] Confirm GitReview clears the **Featured editorial bar**: clear value prop, obvious UVP, high craft, no waitlist/vaporware. Editors judge Useful / Novel / High Craft / Creative. (Only ~10% of submissions get Featured in 2025 — this is the real gate.)
- [ ] Draft all assets: tagline, description, gallery shot list, video script (`01`/`02`).
- [ ] First warm-up post to your audience: "building in public, launching on PH soon."

## 2 weeks out
- [ ] Produce the **gallery images + demo video** (`02-gallery-spec.md`). Upload the video to YouTube; confirm it plays.
- [ ] Build the **`/producthunt` landing page** for PH visitors (restate value prop, the fast-track offer, social proof) so you can attribute PH traffic without UTM-ing the main link.
- [ ] Begin **personal 1:1 outreach** to people who'd genuinely care — early users, dev friends, community members. Ask if they'd like a heads-up on launch day (not "will you upvote").
- [ ] Identify and join (genuinely, ahead of time) the Slack/Discord/X communities you'll share in. Don't show up cold on launch day.
- [ ] Second audience update.

## 1 week out
- [ ] **Finalize and pressure-test the maker's first comment** (`01`). Read it aloud. Have someone else read it.
- [ ] Lock the listing. Triple-check tagline char count (≤60), description, topics, links (clean URL, no UTM).
- [ ] Load-test the app — make sure it survives a traffic spike. Have a rollback ready.
- [ ] Segment your outreach list into the **5 launch-day waves** (`03`): inner circle, Europe, broader EU, US peak, last-call.
- [ ] Write all outreach messages in advance (`05`).
- [ ] Set alarms for 12:01 AM PT. Confirm PST vs PDT.

## 48 hours out
- [ ] Maker's first comment + reply snippets saved in a notes app.
- [ ] "We're live" social posts pre-drafted (X, LinkedIn).
- [ ] Confirm the scheduled launch is set for **12:01 AM PT** on the chosen day.
- [ ] Brief any teammates on comment-reply duty and the no-upvote-asking rule.
- [ ] Rest up.

## What "ready" looks like
- ✅ ~400+ people who'll genuinely engage, reachable by you directly
- ✅ 200–500 email subscribers
- ✅ Gallery + demo video done and uploaded
- ✅ Maker comment written and rehearsed
- ✅ App load-tested, listing locked
- ✅ Outreach segmented into 5 waves, messages pre-written
- ✅ The "large PRs" differentiator carried consistently across every asset
