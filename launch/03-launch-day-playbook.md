# GitReview — Launch Day Playbook (hour by hour)

**Golden rule running through every hour: drive traffic and *comments*, never ask for upvotes.** Link the main post (not an upvote URL). Votes must come from real, established accounts, spread across time and geography.

## The night before
- [ ] Listing 100% locked (tagline, description, gallery, video, topics, links). **Do not edit anything once live.**
- [ ] Maker's first comment finalized in a notes app, ready to paste.
- [ ] Reply snippets (`01-submission.md`) in a notes app.
- [ ] Outreach messages drafted and queued (`05-outreach-templates.md`), segmented into waves.
- [ ] Status page / app warmed up and load-tested — a launch-day outage kills momentum.
- [ ] Sleep. You're up at midnight PT.

## T-0 → 12:01 AM PT — GO LIVE
PH runs a 24-hour cycle anchored to **Pacific time** (PST winter / PDT summer — convert from your timezone). Launching at 12:01 AM PT gives the full 24h to compound and lets you win the early-positioning battle.

- [ ] Publish the launch (or confirm the scheduled launch went live).
- [ ] **Immediately post the maker's first comment.**
- [ ] Verify the page renders correctly (gallery, video, links).

## First hour (12:01–1:00 AM PT) — the most important hour
Positions are won and lost here. Target: **30–100 upvotes from real accounts, plus the first wave of comments.**

- [ ] **Wave 1 outreach** — your closest circle + anyone awake in Europe (daytime) / Asia (evening). Co-founders, close friends, early users, your most engaged followers. Personal 1:1 messages, not a blast.
- [ ] Post your "we're live" announcement on X / LinkedIn (template in `05`). Link the main post.
- [ ] Reply to **every** comment within ~10 minutes. End replies with a question to spur threads. (#1 makers average ~8-min reply times.)

## Hours 1–4 (1:00–4:00 AM PT) — lock in velocity
100+ upvotes in the first 4 hours ≈ ~82% chance of Top 10. Keep momentum unbroken.

- [ ] Continue Wave 1; start **Wave 2** as Europe wakes up (your European contacts, communities, newsletters).
- [ ] Post in relevant Slack/Discord communities you're genuinely part of (template in `05`). "We're live, would love feedback" — never "upvote."
- [ ] Keep replying to every comment within ~10 min.
- [ ] **Don't panic when the visible vote count drops** — PH runs vote-clearing roughly every 2 hours; that's the anti-fraud system working, not a penalty.

## Hours 4–10 (4:00–10:00 AM PT) — European peak
- [ ] **Wave 3** — broader European audience, LinkedIn, indie-hacker communities, any media/newsletter contacts.
- [ ] Share a milestone update on X if it's going well ("We're #2 on Product Hunt 🚀 — thank you") — momentum is contagious. **Never** "we need votes."
- [ ] Keep the comment threads alive — ask commenters follow-up questions.

## Hours 10–16 (10:00 AM–4:00 PM PT) — US peak (the big wave)
This is the largest active-voter window.

- [ ] **Wave 4** — full US audience: email list, X, LinkedIn, every community you're active in.
- [ ] Personally thank notable commenters; engage every thread.
- [ ] If you have a team, divide comment-reply duty so nothing waits >10 min.

## Hours 16–24 (4:00 PM–11:59 PM PT) — final push
- [ ] **Wave 5** — last-call to anyone not yet reached, Asia waking up, time-zone stragglers.
- [ ] A final "last few hours on Product Hunt — thank you all" post (X/LinkedIn). Link the post.
- [ ] Reply to the long tail of comments. Keep going until the cycle ends.

## After the bell (next day)
- [ ] Thank-you post regardless of rank. If you placed, screenshot the badge.
- [ ] **Reply to every remaining comment** — engagement continues to matter for the durable "Top Products" layer.
- [ ] **Start collecting Reviews** (the embeddable "Leave a Review" badge) — Reviews + Shoutouts define PH "Top Products" (top 0.001%) and drive evergreen discovery/SEO. This is the long game, separate from launch-day rank.
- [ ] Fulfill the fast-track offer to everyone who commented their use case.

## Hard don'ts
- ❌ Ask for upvotes anywhere (comment, DM, social, email). → derank/delist risk.
- ❌ Edit the live page during the launch. → resets trending score.
- ❌ Recruit new/throwaway accounts or run vote-trading rings. → stripped + flagged.
- ❌ Use shortened/UTM links on the main URL.
- ❌ Cluster votes from one office/city in one minute.
