# GitReview — Outreach Templates (100% upvote-ask-free)

Every message below asks people to **visit, check out, comment, or give feedback** — never to upvote. This is the line PH enforces: asking for upvotes (or incentivizing them) triggers deranking or delisting. Linking the main post and inviting genuine engagement is explicitly allowed.

**Rules for all outreach:**
- Link the **main PH post**, never an upvote URL or shortened link.
- Personalize 1:1 messages — mass-identical DMs get flagged as "mass messaging."
- Spread sends across the day (the 5 waves in `03`), not all at once.
- Never say "upvote," "vote," "support us with a vote," or offer anything in exchange for a vote.

## What NOT to send (these get you penalized)

- ❌ "Please upvote us on Product Hunt!"
- ❌ "Click here to upvote: [direct upvote link]"
- ❌ "Upvote and I'll give you [anything]"
- ❌ Identical DMs blasted to a large list
- ❌ Anything with a shortened/UTM link to the main post
